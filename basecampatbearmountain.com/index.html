About Base Camp

Base Camp at Bear Mountain will be the first true ski-on, ski-off gated community at Killington—the premier ski resort on the East Coast. The Base Camp townhomes will be brand new, two-story, slope side four bedroom homes with two-car garage parking, home offices, floor-to-ceiling windows and mountain views. Fusing contemporary design with mountain-modern architecture, the Base Camp townhomes will offer high-end finishes and upgrade packages. Prices start at $2,300,000 depending on view and location. With just 11 townhomes remaining in the current offering, be among the first to reserve via the contact form below.

Base Camp at Bear Mountain is being developed by Ottauquechee Realty Advisors, LLC, a trusted associate of Killington/Pico Ski Resort Partners LLC.

Sales Process and Timeline

We are accepting non-binding reservations for the right to enter into a purchase agreement with a $10,000 fully refundable deposit. These reservation agreements will keep you in line for the right to enter into a formal purchase and sale agreement for a specific home of your choice. Construction of the first phase of Base Camp homes is planned to begin in the summer of 2023 with the delivery of completed homes starting in the summer of 2024.

In the summer of 2023, if you wish to move forward under your reservation, we will proceed with the preparation of a formal purchase and sale agreement, which will require a 20% cash deposit along with financial qualification. Selection of your home will be dependent upon the order of your non-binding reservation for a particular home. If the home you desire is not available at the time of selection, you may choose another, or receive a refund of your initial deposit.

Please note that all home plans are subject to change during the construction process.

Around Bear Mountain

The Killington Ski and Summer Resort community is the East Coast’s outdoor adventure capital, offering skiing and snowboarding in the winter as well as now being a major mountain biking destination in the summer. The Killington region offers hiking, kayaking and golfing in the surrounding area and the Killington Adventure Center at the Killington Resort offers summer camps, a ropes course and other activities for children and adults alike.
